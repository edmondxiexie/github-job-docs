Name:
JobHunter: A Job-Search App for ios and Android

Tech:
React Native, Redux, JS

Time:
Jun. 2017

Details:
•	Built an app with a full welcome-screen tutorial for new users, and auth them using Facebook OAuth.
•	Allowed users to search for nearby jobs on map feature based on their location using Indeed’s Job API.
•	Provided an info card list of jobs for users to like or dislike by swiping left or right.
•	Utilized Redux to manage the states of the app making the app behave consistently, and easy to test.
